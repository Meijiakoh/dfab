Final Project Information

My final project is called the Ray fo Light, where it shines bright in the dark and changes colour based on the touch of the sensor. My project aims to motivate users to stay positive and remind users that there is always hope in lif. As the saying goes, there is always light at the end of the tunnel. Besides, the touch on the sensors also changes the screen on the I2C LCD. Quotes can be found on the screen which can constantly remind users to stay positve and keep them motivated. The lamp consists of 5 colours which include green, blue, yellow, magenta and rainbow. The colours and screen displayed will be in 7 states, where the first and final state is when the lights and lcd screen is off.
